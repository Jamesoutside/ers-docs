Mitigation for Stage 2b (Demolition Pre-Permit):
  • Demolition will not commence until Development Permit is in hand — eliminating the below-25% window entirely
  • Development Permit submission is Q1 2026 — permit typically issued within 60–120 days in Dawson Creek
  • Alternatively: ERS can provide additional collateral (personal guarantee, assignment of OSCC grant proceeds, or other assets) to maintain covenant compliance during this period
  • LVCU retains first mortgage over the cleared land — even at $195,000–$235,000, the secured position is preserved; equity shortfall is temporary and self-curing upon permit issuance
