Choose Your Role

Pick what fits your skills and availability. You can have more than one role. All roles are unpaid — this is community work.

📣
High Demand

Community Ambassador

You're the local presence in your community. You spread the word, collect stories, and make sure people know this platform exists.

  • Share the platform in your community
  • Represent your neighbourhood at events
  • Connect people with the platform
  • Report local issues you observe
✍️
High Impact

Story Collector

You find the people whose stories need to be told and help them tell it. With their permission, you document and submit on their behalf.

  • Interview willing community members
  • Write up and submit their stories
  • Photograph with consent
  • Ensure anonymity is protected
🔬
Research

Data Volunteer

Track ED closures, gather data, verify facts. You build the evidence base that makes our submissions bulletproof.

  • Monitor local ED closure incidents
  • Track and document dates and durations
  • Verify statistics for submissions
  • Build community data records
📸
Creative

Content Creator

Photography, video, graphic design, social media. Tell this story visually and help it reach the people who need to see it.

  • Create social media content
  • Photo/video with community members
  • Design infographics and shareable assets
  • Help run our social channels
✉️
High Impact

Letter Writer

We provide the template — you personalize it and send it to your MP. One letter from a real constituent carries more weight than a hundred from a lobbying firm.

  • Send personalized letters to your MP
  • Encourage friends and family to write too
  • Attend MP office hours if available
  • Report back on any responses
🎙️
Media

Podcast & Media Helper

Help produce the BC Healthcare Community podcast and media outreach. Suggest guests, help with logistics, connect us to journalists.

  • Suggest healthcare worker guests
  • Help with interview scheduling
  • Connect us with local journalists
  • Share media with your networks
🏥
Healthcare Only

Healthcare Liaison

For healthcare workers only. You're the bridge between what's happening clinically and what the community and public need to understand.

  • Provide anonymous clinical context
  • Review our submissions for accuracy
  • Connect us with other healthcare workers
  • Advise on what housing would actually work

  • Document outcomes and report back

Why It Matters

🏥

Healthcare workers need a place to live

Every volunteer who helps document the housing crisis makes the case stronger. The stronger the case, the faster the build. The faster the build, the faster workers can come — and stay.

🏛️

Politicians respond to organized communities

891 letters from real BC residents have more political weight than any professional lobbyist. Your letter — personalized, from your community — is political currency.

📺

Media shapes political will

Journalists need sources. Communities need to be seen. Every story collected, every event organized, every piece of content created is another layer of visibility for this crisis.

🌱

This is how grassroots works

It's not a top-down campaign. It's hundreds of people across BC each doing one small thing — and those small things adding up to something that changes healthcare for a generation.
