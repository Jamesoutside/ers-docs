The Exchange

What You Give & What You Get

Membership is a two-way commitment. We're transparent about what we ask of you — and what we deliver in return.

What You Give

  • ⏱️Time — monthly sessions, working groups, events
  • 💰Annual membership dues at your tier level
  • 🧠Expertise — your professional domain knowledge
  • 📣Introductions — warm connections to your network
  • 🏗️Energy — showing up, contributing, building
  • 🤝Trust — a culture of candid, open conversation

What You Get

  • 🏗️First look at every deal, acquisition, and development
  • 📈Co-investment rights and equity participation pathway
  • 🌄Curated experiences — Whistler, Tofino, Vancouver Club
  • 🤝A network that compounds — surgeons, CFOs, developers
  • 📊Full financial transparency — annual reporting, deal memos
  • 🏥Measurable mission impact — units built, workers housed

Your Year as a Founding Member

Here's how your first year unfolds — from introductions to real deal flow.

Phase 1 · Apr–Jun

Foundation

  • May — Mandatory EventFounding dinner at The Vancouver Club. Introductions, structure ratified, first hub presented.
  • June — Online Session 1Working group formation. Deal pipeline introduced. Roles assigned.
Phase 2 · Jul–Sep

Momentum

  • July — Online Session 2Working group updates. Expert guest: healthcare authority relations.
  • August — Online Session 3Deal review. Capital deployment discussion.
  • September — Mandatory EventWhistler retreat. Strategy, pipeline deep dive, group dinner.
Phase 3 · Oct–Dec

Execution

  • October — Site TourOakridge Park or False Creek Flats. Roundtable on what we're building.
  • November — Online Session 4Year review, pipeline status, 2027 planning begins.
  • December — Year-End DinnerBoulevard Kitchen private room. Celebrate progress, announce next cohort.
