Team Structure
Who Runs This

Austin is the founder and vision director. He is not the operator. A lean contracted team runs the day-to-day so Austin can focus on relationships, deals, and direction.

Austin Neill
Founder & Vision Director
Sets the direction. Manages member relationships. Leads quarterly sessions. Drives the hub deal pipeline. Represents the Mastermind in health authority and government conversations. Strategic only — does not manage logistics.
Time: ~5 hrs/week · Annual budget: $12k–$18k (management + expenses)
Event Planner
Contracted · Part-Time
Owns all event logistics. Venue sourcing, booking, RSVPs, catering, run-of-show, on-site execution, post-event wrap. The person who makes every event feel effortless for members.
Time: ~8 hrs/week (event weeks: full-time) · Annual: $8k–$12k
Communications Lead
Part-Time / VA
Monthly newsletter. Session recaps and recordings. Member email sequences. LinkedIn presence for the Mastermind. Branded materials and document management. The voice between events.
Time: ~6 hrs/week · Annual: $3k–$4k
Finance Lead
Member Role or Contracted
Tracks operating budget. Prepares deal summaries for hub investments. Manages member billing. Produces quarterly financial updates. Ideally a Mastermind member (accountant or finance professional) who volunteers or takes a reduced fee.
Time: ~3 hrs/week · Annual: $0 (member) or $3k–$5k (contracted)
Legal Counsel
Retained
Drafts and reviews membership agreements, deal structures, and any government partnership documents. On retainer for deal-specific legal questions. Ideally a Mastermind member (real estate / P3 lawyer) on a preferred rate.
Annual retainer: $3k–$5k · Deal work billed separately
Expert Speakers
Per Session · Pro Bono
The webinar speaker stack (20 profiles, 10 sessions over 2 years) is built on a value-exchange model — no honorarium. Speakers get access to the room, introductions, and amplification. We cover their travel if in-person.
Travel only if required · No honorarium budget needed Year 1
