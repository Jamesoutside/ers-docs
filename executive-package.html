Business Model & Market

01
The Business Model
What We're Doing
Package, Sub-lease & Concierge
ERS sources premium Vancouver units, furnishes them with Janet's procurement, and sub-leases to executives with full white-glove management. We charge what the market will bear and keep the spread.

The core play: we sit between the building and the executive. The building gets a reliable commercial tenant. The executive gets a frictionless home. We capture 50% of the spread.
Why It Works
High Margin. Low Risk. Scalable.
No capital risk: We lease, not own. Lock in the unit, mark up, manage the client.

50% target margin: Market supports it. Executives carry company relocation budgets of $50k–$100k.

Recurring revenue: 3–12 month stays = $18k–$90k+ per placement.

B2B scale path: One corporate HR partnership = 5–20 clients/year. 10 accounts = 50–200 placements annually.
Short-Term Play
High Cash Flow
Sub-lease 3–5 units in Yaletown / Coal Harbour. Each generates $1,200–$2,400/month net. 5 units = $6k–$12k/month passive on top of relocation revenue. Fast to build, low overhead, proven demand.
Long-Term Play
High-Volume B2B Rentals
Become the preferred executive housing provider for Vancouver's major employers — Amazon, Microsoft, KPMG, health authorities, mining HQs. One corporate account = 5–20 placements/year. Scale through relationships, not ads.

The margin isn't from the unit — it's from the service layer. Anyone can rent a Yaletown condo. Nobody is packaging the furniture, concierge, move-in, setup, and ongoing management into one seamless product an executive buys with a single call.
