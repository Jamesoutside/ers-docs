At the close of every project, we host a fully produced appreciation event for your crew. On us.

The Experience

The Evening
How the night runs.
01
Arrival & Mingle
Drinks, appetizers, open patio. The crew unwinds together before sitting down. No agenda yet — just people who've been through it, finally exhaling.
02
The Long Table Dinner
Family-style. Everyone seated together. Warm light, good food, drinks flowing. The MC opens the evening — relaxed, warm, nothing corporate about it.
03
Project Reflection
A guided look back at the full arc. Where it started, what was overcome, what was built. The team sees the whole picture — maybe for the first time.
04
Recognition Moment
Your company presents. We host. Standout contributors are lifted up — the quiet ones, the reliable ones, the people who held it together. This is the part they remember.
05
Gratitude from ERS
We take a moment to thank the workers — not as a vendor, but as a partner. The people who trusted us with their home away from home.
06
The Send-Off
A final toast. A proper goodbye. People leave full, recognized, and proud of what they accomplished. That's what they carry to the next project.
