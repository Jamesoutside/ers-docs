The Problem
BC's healthcare system is struggling to attract and keep the people who run it.

This is one of the most significant challenges facing our province's health authorities today. Attracting and retaining healthcare staff is directly tied to where those people can afford to live — and right now, the housing piece isn't there.

🏥

Hospitals shutting down

Without adequate staff, hospitals across BC are reducing services or closing departments entirely. Communities are left without access to care.

🚫

Failing to attract staff

Healthcare professionals won't relocate to communities where they can't find quality, affordable housing. The talent exists — the infrastructure to support them does not.

🔥

Burnout & isolation

Workers who do relocate often find themselves isolated — no community, no connection. This accelerates burnout and drives early departure from the profession.

📈

Rising administration costs

Health authorities spend heavily on travel, hotels, and agency staff to fill gaps. Purpose-built housing infrastructure dramatically reduces this operational burden.

Healthy living equals stronger communities. When healthcare workers have a true home near where they serve, everything improves — for the worker, the hospital, and the community around them.

Austin Neill · Founder, Elias Range Stays
The Model
Elias Range Stays — proving the concept.

The Healthcare Housing Hub Mastermind runs in parallel with and in support of Elias Range Stays — the operational arm actively proving this model works across BC.

Elias Range Stays is in its active development phase — building isolated examples of the model, refining the approach, and establishing the relationships needed to demonstrate its full value to health authorities. The results are promising. The data is building. The model is real.

The Healthcare Housing Hub Mastermind exists to accelerate this — bringing together the right people, capital, expertise, and relationships to move from isolated examples to a complete, province-wide healthcare housing infrastructure that health authorities can count on.

Joining the Mastermind means being part of building that complete example — and benefiting from the growth of both the Mastermind and Elias Range Stays together.
