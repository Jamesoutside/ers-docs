Email Cadence — Weekly Rhythm
Week Action Recipient(s) Owner
Week 1 Re-engagement email — where are we, what do you need, what are we working on. Warm opener, no pressure. Steve Raper + Björn Petersson Austin sends
Week 2 Region-by-region housing brief. Ask what their current data shows. Open joint problem definition door. Steve Raper Siavash builds, Austin sends

Week 4 Full Service Package preview + request for a next call. "We saw your 2023 board ask. Here's what we've built." Steve Raper Austin sends
Week 5+ Rotate through tracks — one meaningful touchpoint per week, per track. Always include something of value. Varies by track Siavash manages cadence

Campaign Email Rules

  • Every email moves toward a specific result — a meeting, a document review, an intro, a decision
  • No check-ins for the sake of check-ins
  • Always include something of value — data, a brief, a document, a finding
  • Track open/responses — if no reply in 7 days, follow up once
  • Never send the same email twice — each touchpoint advances the conversation
