Cap Rate Rationale — 7.25%: Standard Dawson Creek commercial cap rates are 7.5–9.0%. A downward adjustment to 7.25% is justified on the basis of: (1) institutional-grade tenancy with Northern Health as anchor, (2) federal OSCC grant backing reducing project risk, (3) purpose-built nature of the asset with a defined long-term demand base, and (4) City of Dawson Creek formal endorsement reducing regulatory risk. This rate will be supported by the "as complete" appraisal required as a condition precedent by LVCU.

Section 11 — Post-Construction Refinancing Path (CMHC MLI Select)
