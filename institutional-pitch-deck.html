BC Strategic Alignment

ERS Is Built for BC's Current Healthcare Infrastructure Moment

BC is investing billions in healthcare infrastructure. ERS is the workforce housing layer that makes those investments yield their full return.

🏗️ BC Builds Program — $2.95B
The province's historic housing commitment recognizes that stable, affordable housing is critical infrastructure. ERS is the healthcare-specialized operator that deploys within this framework — purpose-built for clinical workforce needs, not general rental housing.
🏥 GoHealth BC — Growing Fast, Still Dependent on Hotels
GoHealth BC tripled its workforce in 24 months. Yet as of September 2024, Northern Health is still consuming 55,000 agency hours/month — because GoHealth staff are in hotels, not homes. ERS provides the stable housing that converts GoHealth placements into permanent community anchors.
🏚️ Hospital Construction Wave — Housing Demand Is Certain
New hospitals are under construction or planned across BC: Dawson Creek & District ($590M), Mills Memorial Terrace ($635M already open), UHNBC Prince George ($103M expansion), Burnaby Hospital, Surrey Pretoria, Cowichan District. Every new hospital creates immediate workforce housing demand. ERS is planning ahead.
📋 Board-Level Housing Priority — The Door Is Open
In 2025, Northern Health formally elevated housing to a board-level strategic priority. "Housing & Childcare" was listed alongside GoHealth BC in recruitment strategy documentation in June 2024. The health authority is actively looking for a housing partner. ERS is that partner.

"BC Builds, GoHealth BC, and the hospital construction wave have all created the conditions for ERS to operate at scale. The province has funded the demand. We provide the supply."
