The ERS Model — How We Work

Full-Service Workforce Housing | For Healthcare & Construction Organizations

Executive Overview
"On a major construction project, every trade contractor manages their own workforce housing — separately, expensively, and without coordination. That cost gets baked into every contract and inflates the total project budget. ERS is the housing contractor for the whole project — one entity that sources, manages, and delivers housing across every contractor group, at a fraction of the fragmented cost."
🏗️
The Traditional Approach
Every contractor manages their own.
  • ✕ Each contractor sources & pays for housing independently
  • ✕ Workers find their own — or land in expensive hotels
  • ✕ High per-night cost becomes a line item in every contract
  • ✕ No coordination between trades — duplicate costs, gaps in supply
  • ✕ Housing overhead inflates the total project budget
  • ✕ Zero added value — just a cost to manage
✅
The ERS Approach
One housing contractor. Every crew.
  • ✓ One housing contract covers all trades and all crews
  • ✓ Reduced per-person cost — shared infrastructure, no duplication
  • ✓ Streamlined admin — each contractor's housing line item shrinks
  • ✓ Ready-to-live units — furnished, stocked, managed
  • ✓ 24/7 guest services for every worker on the project
  • ✓ Adds value beyond beds — community, retention, wellbeing

ERS is the dedicated housing contractor for the project — the same way a project hires a single mechanical contractor or a single electrical contractor. We source all the housing options, manage all the suppliers, and deliver coordinated housing services across every group on site. The result: lower cost per person, less admin for each contractor, and a workforce that arrives to a home — not a problem to solve.

Behind the Scenes

The Three Service Chains

Every ERS engagement is powered by three integrated chains operating simultaneously. You see the result — your workers in a home. We manage everything that makes it happen.

🔍
Chain 01

Sourcing & Inventory

ERS identifies, negotiates, and acquires existing housing units in target markets — apartment buildings, houses, suites — and brings them into our managed inventory. We handle all landlord relationships, lease negotiations, and unit qualification.

  • Market-by-market property sourcing
  • Lease negotiation and management
  • Unit qualification (location, condition, capacity)
  • Inventory growth planning by demand signals
🏠
Chain 02

Property Management & Guest Services

Once units are in our inventory, ERS manages everything — furnishing, maintenance, cleaning, guest onboarding, and 24/7 support for every resident. Your workers arrive to a home, not a room.

  • Full furnishing and setup (ready-to-live standard)
  • Professional cleaning and maintenance programs
  • Guest check-in, check-out, and 24/7 support line
  • Incident management and resolution
  • Quality assurance and property standards
🏗️
Chain 03

New Development & Custom Solutions

Where existing inventory doesn't meet demand, ERS builds it. From purpose-built workforce housing developments to temporary modular solutions, ERS can develop, finance, and operate new housing assets adjacent to hospitals, construction sites, and industrial corridors.

  • Purpose-built workforce housing developments
  • Temporary and modular housing solutions
  • Hospital-adjacent and site-adjacent development
  • First Nations equity development partnerships
  • Construction financing and capital structuring
